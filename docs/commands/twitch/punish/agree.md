## Punish Agree

Agree to join the Punishment Wheel.

### Use
- !punish agree @discordUsername

### Prerequisite
Not currently signed up for the Punishment Wheel.

### Expected Behavior
Responds to let you know you have been signed up for the Punishment Wheel.
