## No Context Harry Potter
Posts random line from Harry Potter book 1

### Use
- !nchp
- !NoContextHP
- !nocontexthp
- !NCHP

### Expected behavior
Posts message in Twitch chat of a random line from Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone.
